Review of the movie V For Vendetta (2006)

Brian Pereira, January 29th, 2024

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

V for Vendetta is a thrilling movie filled with great action sequences along with an in-depth storyline that will make you question the world around you. In this movie, the main charater, V, is a human with exceptional physical and mental gifts that he unleashes to take down a corrupt authoritarian government. As his plan unfolds, he encounters Evey and with her help realizes that even though he is driven by vengence, his overall goal is noble. I really enjoy this movie because it has its violent action but also has a great script wih quotes from past great writers. The movie also pulls at your heart strings in multiple ways to get you more invested in it.

V for Vendetta Trivia

  • Trivia #1:The domino scene, where V tips over black and red dominoes to form a giant letter V, involved 22,000 dominoes. It took four professional domino assemblers 200 hours to set it up.
  • Trivia #2: Evey mentions to Gordon Dietrich that "eggie in the basket" is the same breakfast V made for her the first morning she spent at his place. She fails to mention (or doesn't notice) that Dietrich and V also greeted her in exactly the same way, not with "good morning", but with "bonjour mademoiselle". Also, Stan Getz is playing in the background in both instances.
  • Trivia #3: The cast and crew were only allowed to shoot near the British Parliament and Big Ben (the clock tower) from midnight to 4:30 a.m., and they could only stop traffic for four minutes at a time.
